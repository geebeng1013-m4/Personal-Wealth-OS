## Style Prompt

Calm educational documentary timeline with warm natural-light illustrations, tactile paper grain, restrained museum-exhibit typography, and slow cinematic motion. The piece should feel reflective and humane rather than technical or sensational.

## Colors

- Background umber: `#2f2a24`
- Warm paper: `#f0dec0`
- Ink: `#27231f`
- Clay accent: `#c77f4f`
- Sage accent: `#6f9287`

## Typography

- Headlines: Georgia, 900 weight, large and editorial.
- Supporting text: Arial, 300-400 weight, clear and quiet.

## Motion

- Gentle blur crossfades between scenes.
- Slow image drift and scale, like a camera breathing.
- Text enters with soft vertical movement and opacity.

## What NOT to Do

- No neon, sci-fi grids, or aggressive cuts.
- No crowded diagrams or tiny text.
- No jump cuts between eras.
